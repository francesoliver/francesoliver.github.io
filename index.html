Frances Morgan Oliver

IN A NUTSHELL

Frances is an experienced businessperson with a creative background, who excels at being self-directed and taking on the unfamiliar without hesitation. Dedicated to making sure business operations run smoothly through prioritizing and using analytical and communication skills. Proven ability to manage and execute projects by leading clear communication between parties and maintaining impeccable organization. Always one step ahead, recognized for improving processes and efficiency across multiple positions.

Frances hails from Connecticut and went to school in Chicago, where she studied Arts Management and Entrepreneurship at Columbia College and honed in on her operational skills through years in office management and event planning. Never the one to like being placed in a box, she sought out a new kind of role when her husband took a job in Charleston in 2015. Landing a position as head of product development at Sideshow Press, it gave her the opportunity to combine her love and talent for artistic endeavours with her business background.

Some of the objects I've brought to life have appeared at Starbucks, J.Crew, BHLDN, and many small boutiques around the country. Click here to see a few examples of my work.

Outside of work, Frances spends time restoring her 1930’s home, perusing the antique stores for rare costume jewelry, cooking, and dreaming of owning a sailboat one day.
